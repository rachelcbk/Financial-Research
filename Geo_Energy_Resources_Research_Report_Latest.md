## Company Overview

**Geo Energy Resources Limited** is a Singapore-based investment holding company engaged in coal mining, production, and trading. The company operates primarily in Indonesia through its subsidiaries.

### Business Segments:

1. **Coal Mining (Primary Revenue Driver)**
   - Production and sale of coal from owned mines
   - Mining concessions in East Kalimantan and South Kalimantan
   - Key assets: PT Bumi Enggang Khatulistiwa (4,570 hectares), PT Sungai Danau Jaya, PT Tanah Bumbu Resources

2. **Coal Trading**
   - Purchase and sale of coal from third parties
   - Operations in China, Indonesia, South Korea, India, Vietnam, and Philippines

3. **Mining Services**
   - Mining contracting and project management
